FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a mini-split air conditioner system?

A mini-split air conditioner, also called a ductless system, has an outdoor unit (condenser) and indoor unit (evaporators). It provides cooling and heating to specific areas in a home or building.

How is a mini-split AC different from a central AC system?

Central AC systems requires ducts and distribute air throughout the whole home whereas mini-split AC operates without ducts and has air flow in specific areas.

How long does a mini-split AC installation take?

The duration of a mini-split AC installation depends on how many you want installed. On average, a single-zone installation can take about 3 hours, while multi-zone installations may require additional time.

Is there warranty?

We have a one-year warranty. It covers any defects in materials or workmanship that may arise during normal use of the mini-split air conditioner.

What is the maintenance for a mini-split air conditioner?

Regular maintenance includes cleaning or replacing air filters, cleaning the indoor/outdoor units, and checking refrigerant levels for proper airflow.

How long does mini-split AC systems last?

With proper care and maintenance, a mini-split AC system can be expected to last between 10 to 15 years on average.
